Some population-genetic statistics

If you followed the basic simulation section you'll know that random inheritance patterns in the population cause haplotypes to be lost over time, and others to increase in frequency. As a result genetic diversity is lost.

Key popgen metrics​

Let's use hih_ihi​ be mean the haplotype of individual iii, and hi(l)h_{i}(l)hi​(l) to mean the allele carried by haplotype hih_ihi​ at SNP lll. And I(⋅)I(\cdot)I(⋅) will denote the indicator function, which is 111 or 000 according to whether the condition is true.

  • The heterozygosity HHH. This is the probability that two individuals drawn at random carry different haplotypes:
